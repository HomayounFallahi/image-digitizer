Quick Help

This app converts graph image pixels into real numeric data points.

  • Upload a graph image.
  • In Calibration mode, click C1-C4 and enter their real values, then press Calibrate.
  • Choose X-Axis Type and Y-Axis Type (Linear, Log, ln, or Inverse) to match your chart scale.
  • Switch to Digitize mode and click points to extract P1, P2, and more.
  • Enable Magnifier for precision clicking; red guides help align the cursor.
  • Sort, connect, undo, clear, and export data as CSV or JSON.
